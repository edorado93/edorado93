Surrounded by long stretches of coastal beaches and towering evergreen trees, @visitkalalochlodge offers visitors a cozy retreat in Olympic National Park. Spend your days tidepooling, exploring the nearby rain forest, hiking, relaxing by the fire, and watching the perfect sunset after enjoying the fresh, local cuisine at their onsite restaurant.
