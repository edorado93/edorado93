In 1936, the @washingtonrowing men’s team won gold at the Olympics in Berlin. Many know this captivating story from the celebrated book “The Boys in the Boat.” Watch that story come to life and learn more about the current rowing program on a tour through Washington Rowing’s Conibear Shellhouse and the 1918 World War I airplane hangar known to the “Boys” as the ASUW Shell House. Visit washingtonrowing.com/history/tours to book today and learn more!
